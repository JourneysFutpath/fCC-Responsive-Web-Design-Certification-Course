Hi, I'm Journey

I'm a web developer based out of north east Florida, USA who is passionate about helping people and solving problems. I have an insatiable thirst for knowledge and work daily towards developing new skills to add to my tool belt. Doing so has afforded me the ability to make aesthetically pleasing, creative & useful bespoke solutions. I'm a firm believer that the details matter just as much as the big picture when it comes to my clients. Striving to not only meet, but exceed their expectations by providing superior products & service.

During my down-time, I enjoy working on my online journal , being an avid commentator on issues and current events affecting the United States of America across all forms of social media, in addition to bringing a fresh perspective to the world of news and media by cracking into the world of journalism.

I'd like to welcome you to check out my newest project , and honored if you would take the time to provide me constructive critism and feedback on some of my previous work found in my in my gallery .

If you'd like to get in touch, please contact me for business inquiries or just to say hello!
